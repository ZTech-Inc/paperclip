# Revenue Model

ZTech Incorporation generates revenue through four complementary streams, each designed to create recurring relationships with clients while maintaining alignment between cost and value delivered. The model is built on a zero-loss operating principle: every engagement must cover its direct costs (AI compute, tooling, infrastructure) and contribute to fixed cost coverage from day one.

---

## 1. Revenue Streams

### 1.1 Monthly Retainers (Recurring Revenue)

Retainers are the foundation of ZTech's revenue model. Clients subscribe to ongoing service tiers that provide continuous AI agent workforce access.

**Structure:**

| Service Line | Starter | Growth | Enterprise |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI Automation | $2,000 - $5,000/mo | $5,000 - $15,000/mo | $15,000 - $50,000+/mo |
| Digital Marketing | $1,500 - $3,500/mo | $3,500 - $10,000/mo | $10,000 - $30,000+/mo |
| Blockchain Development | $15,000 - $40,000/mo | $15,000 - $40,000/mo | Custom |
| Bundled (2+ services) | 10% discount | 15% discount | 20% discount |

**What retainers include:**
- Dedicated Paperclip Company instance with assigned agent team
- Agreed-upon scope of work per month (number of automations, campaigns, or features)
- Dashboard access with real-time progress and cost visibility
- Weekly automated progress reports from the CEO agent
- Defined SLA for response time and task completion

**Revenue recognition:** Monthly, on the 1st of each billing cycle. Net-30 payment terms for Enterprise; prepaid for Starter and Growth.

**Target mix:** 60-70% of total revenue by Year 2. Retainers provide predictable MRR and reduce client acquisition pressure.

### 1.2 Project Fees (One-Time Revenue)

Fixed-price and time-and-materials engagements for discrete deliverables with defined scope and timeline.

**Fixed-Price Projects:**

| Service | Typical Scope | Price Range |
|---|---|---|
| Smart Contract Development | Single contract or small set | $10,000 - $30,000 |
| DeFi Protocol | Full protocol with frontend | $50,000 - $200,000+ |
| NFT Platform | Minting + marketplace | $20,000 - $75,000 |
| Tokenization Framework | RWA or security token | $30,000 - $100,000 |
| Smart Contract Audit | Per contract, complexity-based | $5,000 - $25,000 |
| AI Automation Build (standalone) | Custom workflow or chatbot | $8,000 - $25,000 |
| Analytics Infrastructure Setup | GA4, dashboards, attribution | $5,000 - $15,000 |

**Time-and-Materials (T&M):**
- Used for projects with uncertain scope (R&D, exploratory builds, complex integrations)
- Billed at blended rate of $150 - $250/hour based on agent tier deployed
- Weekly time reports generated from Paperclip's run history and cost tracking
- Budget caps agreed upfront with the client; auto-pause at cap via Paperclip's budget enforcement

**Revenue recognition:** Milestone-based for fixed-price (30% upfront, 40% at mid-point, 30% at delivery). Monthly billing for T&M.

**Target mix:** 25-35% of total revenue. Project fees serve as the entry point -- clients start with a project, then convert to retainers.

### 1.3 Performance Bonuses (Variable Revenue)

Performance bonuses align ZTech's compensation with client outcomes. They are negotiated per engagement and tied to measurable KPIs.

**Bonus Structures:**

| Metric | Trigger | Typical Bonus |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue increase | Client revenue growth attributable to ZTech's work exceeds target | 5-15% of incremental revenue above baseline |
| Cost reduction | Automation reduces operational costs below agreed threshold | 10-20% of documented savings in first 12 months |
| ROAS | Paid ad campaigns exceed target ROAS | 5-10% of ad spend savings vs. benchmark |
| Time-to-market | Delivery ahead of agreed timeline | $2,000 - $10,000 flat bonus per milestone delivered early |
| Token launch success | Token/NFT launch exceeds volume or price targets | 1-3% of launch proceeds |

**Rules:**
- Bonuses are always in addition to base fees (retainer or project), never in lieu of them
- Metrics must be independently measurable (analytics dashboards, on-chain data, client financial reports)
- Bonus caps are agreed upfront -- no uncapped variable compensation
- Payout frequency: quarterly, with a 30-day reconciliation period

**Target mix:** 5-10% of total revenue. Performance bonuses increase client confidence and improve retention.

### 1.4 Licensing Fees (Product Revenue)

As ZTech builds reusable AI tools, automation templates, and agent configurations during client work, these are productized and licensed.

**Licensing Products:**

| Product | Description | Pricing Model |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-built automation templates | Customer support chatbot, invoice processor, lead qualifier, reporting pipeline | $500 - $2,000/month per template |
| Industry-specific agent teams | Pre-configured Paperclip Company with agents tailored for e-commerce, fintech, healthcare, etc. | $3,000 - $8,000/month |
| Custom AI model access | Trained ML models (churn predictor, demand forecaster) deployed as API endpoints | $1,000 - $5,000/month based on API call volume |
| Audit report templates | Standardized smart contract audit framework and reporting | $2,000 - $5,000 per audit (tool license) |

**Build strategy:** Every client engagement should produce at least one reusable component. The engineering team tags reusable artifacts during development, and the product team packages them quarterly.

**Target mix:** 5-10% of total revenue in Year 1, growing to 15-20% by Year 3 as the product library matures.

## 2. Pricing Logic and Scaling

### 2.1 Cost-Plus Pricing Foundation

Every engagement is priced to cover:

1. **Direct AI compute costs** -- Token usage across all agents assigned to the client (tracked per-agent, per-month in Paperclip)
2. **Infrastructure costs** -- Proportional share of Paperclip hosting, database, storage, and monitoring
3. **Tooling costs** -- API subscriptions (analytics, ad platforms, blockchain RPCs, design tools)
4. **Margin** -- Target gross margin of 65-75% on retainers, 50-60% on projects

### 2.2 How Pricing Scales with Client Size

| Client Size | Typical Engagement | Agent Team Size | Monthly AI Compute Cost | Target Monthly Revenue |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Solo founder / micro-business | 1 service, Starter tier | 2-3 agents | $200 - $500 | $2,000 - $5,000 |
| Small business (10-50 employees) | 1-2 services, Growth tier | 5-8 agents | $500 - $2,000 | $5,000 - $15,000 |
| Mid-market (50-200 employees) | 2-3 services, Enterprise tier | 10-20 agents | $2,000 - $8,000 | $15,000 - $50,000 |
| Large Enterprise / DeFi protocol | Full-stack engagement | 20-50 agents | $5,000 - $20,000 | $50,000 - $150,000+ |

**Scaling principle:** As client complexity increases, agent team size grows linearly while coordination overhead grows sub-linearly (Paperclip handles the coordination). This creates improving unit economics at higher tiers.

### 2.3 Bundling Discounts

Clients engaging multiple service lines receive bundled pricing:

| Bundle | Discount | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| 2 service lines | 10% | Reduced client acquisition cost amortized across services |
| 3 service lines | 15-20% | Full-stack engagement with highest retention probability |
| Annual commitment (any tier) | Additional 10% | Predictable revenue; lower churn risk |

## 3. Billing Code Tracking and Cross-Team Cost Allocation

### 3.1 Paperclip-Native Cost Tracking

Every dollar of AI compute is tracked automatically through Paperclip's cost management system:

**Per-Agent Cost Tracking:**
- Each agent reports cost events after every heartbeat: provider, model, input/output tokens, cost in cents
- Costs are aggregated per agent per month with real-time dashboard visibility
- Budget enforcement auto-pauses agents at 100% monthly spend -- no cost overruns

**Per-Project Cost Tracking:**
- Every task (Issue) is linked to a Project and a Goal
- Paperclip's cost-by-project API aggregates all agent costs for tasks within a project
- This maps directly to client billing: Project = Client Engagement

**Per-Client Billing Code Structure:**
```
Client: Acme Corp
  Project: AI Chatbot Build (Fixed Price: $20,000)
    Agent: Backend Engineer -- $450 compute this month
    Agent: AI Specialist -- $800 compute this month
    Agent: QA Engineer -- $200 compute this month
    Total direct cost: $1,450
    Gross margin: 92.8%

  Project: Monthly Marketing Retainer ($8,000/month)
    Agent: Content Writer -- $300 compute
    Agent: SEO Specialist -- $250 compute
    Agent: PPC Manager -- $400 compute
    Agent: Analytics Lead -- $150 compute
    Total direct cost: $1,100
    Gross margin: 86.3%
```

### 3.2 Cross-Team Work Cost Allocation

When an agent from one department works on another department's project (e.g., an engineer helping with a marketing automation build), Paperclip tracks this automatically:

- The task's `projectId` determines which client/project absorbs the cost
- The agent's `reportsTo` chain determines organizational ownership
- Cross-team tasks are flagged in Paperclip -- the cost follows the project, not the department

**Internal cost allocation rules:**
1. Agent compute costs are always attributed to the project the task belongs to
2. If an agent works on multiple projects in a month, costs are split proportionally by task
3. Shared infrastructure costs (Paperclip hosting, monitoring) are allocated as a percentage of total compute cost
4. Internal (non-billable) work is tracked under a dedicated "Internal Operations" project per billing period

### 3.3 Client-Facing Cost Reports

Every client receives monthly cost reports generated from Paperclip data:

- **Task completion summary** -- What was done, by which agent, status of each deliverable
- **Cost breakdown** -- AI compute costs per agent and project (full transparency)
- **Budget utilization** -- How much of the agreed budget was consumed
- **Value delivery metrics** -- Mapping costs to outcomes (tasks completed, features shipped, campaigns launched)

This radical transparency is a competitive differentiator. Clients see exactly what they are paying for, building trust and reducing churn.

## 4. Revenue Model Summary

| Stream | Year 1 Target Mix | Year 3 Target Mix | Pricing Basis |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly Retainers | 45% | 60% | Tiered subscription by service and scale |
| Project Fees | 40% | 20% | Fixed-price or T&M with milestone billing |
| Performance Bonuses | 10% | 10% | KPI-triggered variable compensation |
| Licensing Fees | 5% | 10% | Monthly subscription for productized tools |

**Key financial guardrails:**
- No engagement below 50% gross margin
- No uncapped variable compensation
- Every client engagement must reach positive gross margin by Month 2
- Agent budget enforcement prevents compute cost overruns
- Monthly financial review against projections with course correction
